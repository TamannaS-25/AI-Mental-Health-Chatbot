Immediate Coping Techniques

If you are experiencing panic, heavy anxiety, or severe stress right now, try this somatic grounding exercise:

5 Identify 5 things you can see around you (e.g. a book, a plant, a desk, a light).
4 Identify 4 things you can touch (e.g. your keyboard, chair fabric, your hair, the cold desk).
3 Identify 3 things you can hear (e.g. a hum of a computer fan, wind blowing outside, remote cars).
2 Identify 2 things you can smell (e.g. soap, coffee, fresh rain).
1 Identify 1 thing you can taste or notice in your mouth.
